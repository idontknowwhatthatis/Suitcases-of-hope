Our Mission

Suitcases of Hope is a nonprofit based in La Vergne, Tennessee, founded by Cailey Shae Martin. We believe no child entering the foster system should have to carry their belongings in a trash bag. We deliver high-quality suitcases filled with hygiene products, blankets, journals, stuffed animals, and other comfort items to children entering or transitioning within the foster care system.
